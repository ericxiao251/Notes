6. Keyboard shortcuts:

	|FOR  |ACTION                      |
	|-----|----------------------------|
	|⌘D   |Selects highlighted word    |
	|⌘→   |Goes to end of the line     |
	|⌘←   |Goes to start of the line   |
	|⌥→   |Goes to end of next word    |
	|⌥←   |Goes to start of next word  |
	|^⌘↑  |Moves selected lines up     |
	|^⌘↓  |Moves selected lines down   |
